Rational

We are choosing option 1 because of its advanced communication capabilities. It has ample connectivity, with 34 GPIO pins. There are two MSSP modules that can be configured for I2C or SPI which would be perfect because our subsystems require the use of both I2C and SPI. Additionally, there are also two modules for EUSART. It has about the same characteristics as the other two options, but option 1, the PIC18F47J13-I/PT, has more expanded capabilities and most importantly, meets Peralta manufacturing requirements.
